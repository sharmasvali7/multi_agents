TechMart Electronics — Shipping Policy

1. Delivery Timelines
Standard shipping takes 3-6 business days depending on your location. Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune) typically receive orders within 2-4 business days. Remote areas may take up to 8 business days.

2. Shipping Charges
Orders above ₹999 qualify for free standard shipping. Orders below this threshold incur a flat ₹49 shipping fee. TechMart Premium members get free express shipping on all orders regardless of value.

3. Express Shipping
Express delivery (1-2 business days) is available in select metro cities for an additional ₹149, or free for Premium members.

4. Order Processing Time
Orders are processed and dispatched within 24 hours on business days. Orders placed after 6 PM or on Sundays/public holidays are processed the next business day.

5. Delivery Attempts
Our courier partners attempt delivery up to 3 times. If all attempts fail, the package is returned to our warehouse and a refund is initiated automatically minus shipping costs, unless a redelivery is requested.

6. Address Changes
Delivery address can be changed only before the order is dispatched. Once dispatched, address changes are not possible; you may refuse delivery and place a new order instead.

7. Lost or Delayed Shipments
If tracking shows no movement for more than 5 days, contact support immediately. We investigate with the courier partner and issue a refund or replacement if the shipment is confirmed lost.
