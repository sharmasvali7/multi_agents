TechMart Electronics — Warranty Policy

1. Standard Warranty
All electronics sold by TechMart come with a manufacturer warranty ranging from 6 months to 2 years, depending on the product category. The exact duration is listed on each product page.

2. What Is Covered
Manufacturing defects, hardware malfunctions under normal use, battery defects (for the first 6 months), and screen issues not caused by physical damage are covered.

3. What Is Not Covered
Physical damage, liquid damage, unauthorized repairs, theft, loss, damage from unapproved third-party chargers/accessories, and normal wear and tear are not covered under standard warranty.

4. Extended Warranty
TechMart offers an Extended Warranty Plan purchasable within 15 days of buying a product, extending coverage by an additional 1 or 2 years, and adding one-time accidental damage protection.

5. Claiming Warranty Service
To claim warranty service, go to "My Orders," select the product, click "Request Warranty Service," and describe the issue. You may be asked to ship the product to our service center or visit an authorized service partner, depending on the product category.

6. Repair vs Replacement
TechMart or its service partner will first attempt repair. If the product cannot be repaired within 15 business days, a replacement or full refund will be issued instead.

7. Warranty Transfer
Standard warranty is tied to the original invoice and is transferable to a new owner if the invoice is provided; it does not need to be re-registered.
