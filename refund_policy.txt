TechMart Electronics — Refund & Returns Policy

1. Eligibility
Products can be returned within 10 days of delivery if they are unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice. Electronics that have been physically damaged by the customer, or have had seals broken on software/activation codes, are not eligible for return.

2. Refund Timeline
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount is credited to the original payment method. For UPI and net banking payments, funds typically reflect within 2-3 additional business days depending on your bank.

3. Refund for Cancelled Orders
If an order is cancelled before it ships, a full refund is issued within 24-48 hours automatically, with no return shipment required.

4. Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued if an item is returned without original accessories (charger, box, manual) or shows minor signs of use, at the discretion of our quality inspection team.

5. Non-Refundable Items
Gift cards, TechMart Wallet top-ups, extended warranty plans after 15 days of purchase, and customized/engraved products are non-refundable.

6. How to Initiate a Refund
Go to "My Orders," select the item, click "Return or Refund," choose a reason, and schedule a pickup. Our AI assistant can also initiate this process directly in chat if you provide your order ID.

7. Refund for Defective Products
If a product arrives damaged or defective, TechMart covers the full refund or replacement, including return shipping costs, provided it is reported within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage.
